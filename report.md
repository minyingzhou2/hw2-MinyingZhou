## Workflow
Summarizing meetings into action items

## User
Business professionals / team members who attend meetings

## Input
Unstructured meeting notes or transcripts (e.g., raw notes, bullet points, or partial transcripts)

## Output
A structured summary including:
- Key discussion points
- Action items
- Owners (if mentioned)
- Deadlines (if mentioned)

## Value
This workflow saves time by converting messy meeting notes into clear, actionable summaries. It improves team alignment, ensures accountability, and reduces the risk of missing important follow-ups.
